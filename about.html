About Us


History

Founded in 2012, Voyager has grown to 4 project teams and over 30 members. In that time Voyager has completed projects for a number of leading companies, including Amazon, Instagram and the NBA. Alumni have brought those experiences to prestigious firms such as Bain & Company, Deloitte, Google, Apple and more.



Projects

Every semester Voyager works with clients on 4 different projects for 10-12 weeks. Projects are headed by a project leader and strategy advisor with a team of 4 to 5 consultants. Alumni and faculty mentors also play a role when appropriate.



Culture

The Voyager team is a close group of individuals bonded by shared personal and professional pursuits. New members connect over a weekend retreat and regular weekly socials such as happy hours, team dinners, concerts, kick-backs and more.



People

Aside from simply working together, our consultants also grow together. Despite a varied academic background including but not limited to Economics, Computer Science, Business Administration, Statistics, Rhetoric and Public Policy, and Mechanical Engineering, members share a passion for their work that is transformed into lifelong friendships.



Impact

The key to Voyager’s growth has been a tireless work ethic to support the right strategy with the appropriate data no matter the size or difficulty of the problem. Each team is built specifically around the client and strives to craft a solution that will not only inform a client decision but make a measurable impact towards a client’s goal.




About

Voyager Consulting is a student-run, business organization that provides strategy consulting services to growth stage and Fortune 500 companies.
