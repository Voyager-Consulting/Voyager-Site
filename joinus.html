Lifelong Friendships

Although Voyager members can come from varied backgrounds, our shared passions and values contribute to a strong social culture. New consultants bond with the club through our retreats, banquets, concerts, kick-backs and other weekly socials.

Authentic Experience

Voyager's client and project selection each semester is unparalleled in terms of variety and authenticity. Over the course of their time at Voyager, consultants will be able work on a comprehensive set of projects from social media clients to multinational retail corporations.

Personal Growth

As an organization, we are committed to helping our consultants develop both professionally and emotionally. Our new members participate in a carefully planned training plan as soon as they are accepted to the team. Our members become collaborative team members, effective communicators and assertive leaders throughout their time in Voyager.

About

Voyager Consulting is a student-run, business organization that provides strategy consulting services to growth stage and Fortune 500 companies.
