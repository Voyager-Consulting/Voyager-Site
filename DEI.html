Impact Projects

Each semester, Voyager Consulting chooses a project based on the positive impact that it will have. This initiative has allowed us to work with nonprofit organizations and corporations to conduct research and develop strategic recommendations to increase their outreach, as well as providing members with the opportunity to give back to the larger community.


Mastercard

The Mastercard project dove into the company’s Community Pass Platform — an initiative that delivers digital identity management and multi-wallet services to underserved communities around the world. In addition to providing marketing/documentation recommendations to Mastercard, our team also built the basis for two reliant apps that Mastercard can share, resulting in a more approachable onboarding experience for such marginalized individuals.

Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

The BMGF project focused on scoping out and segmenting the issue of substandard and falsified medicines in lower-middle income countries, identifying available evidence as well as knowledge gaps regarding SFM prevalence, and finally performing in-depth research on major ongoing initiatives from organizations that the Foundation could potentially support.

Nielsen

On behalf of our client, Nielsen, our team built a recruitment pipeline for underprivileged and underrepresented students at Oakland Technical High School. The outreach program successfully cultivated an early interest in technology and still directly addresses barriers that high schoolers face when entering the STEM field.

American Red Cross

Developed a marketing strategy to grow safety training within the small business channel by conducting a transaction analysis, researching customer behavior. Created recommendations to grow scalable B2B lead generation programs and overall engagement.

This breadth of knowledge of our members allow us to go above and beyond on our projects, as our members are able to use their diverse academic interests and majors, personal passions, and a global perspective.

Donations

The members of Voyager Consulting find it incredibly important to give back to our community and support various nonprofit organizations. In order to do this, we provide monetary donations to a multiple charities that are chosen by and supported by our members.


Charity: Water

Charity: Water, founded in 2006, is a nonprofit organization that brings clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. It currently operates in 29+ countries and has funded 78,000 water projects serving over 13 million people.

Larkin Street Youth Services

Larkin Street Youth Services is a nonprofit empowering young people to move beyond homelessness. They’ve helped over 75,000 young adults in San Francisco by providing healthcare, housing, employment, and education services. They connect young adults to a spectrum of services designed to help young people develop the knowledge and skills for adulthood.

St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital

St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital is at the forefront of defeating childhood cancer and other life-threatening diseases. Families never receive a bill from St. Jude for treatment and procedures invented at St. Jude has helped push the overall childhood cancer survival rate from 20 percent to over 80 percent.

Feeding Pets of the Homeless

Feeding Pets of the Homeless is the first and one of the few national nonprofits providing pet food and emergency veterinary care to pets that belong to homeless people.

Action Against Hunger

Action Against Hunger is a global humanitarian organization that takes decisive action against the causes and effects of hunger. Last year, AAH served more than 25 million people in nearly 50 countries, providing food security, water sanitation, and healthcare services.
